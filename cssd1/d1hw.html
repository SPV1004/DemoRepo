John F. Kennedy

About Me

I am John Fitzgerald Kennedy, the 35th President of the United States, serving from 1961 until my assassination in 1963. Known for my leadership during pivotal moments such as the Cuban Missile Crisis, I advocated for civil rights, space exploration, and a vision of public service that inspired a generation. My presidency is often remembered for its sense of hope, energy, and commitment to progress.
