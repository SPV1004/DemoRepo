Toyota Land Cruzer LC300 in a Glances

The Toyota Land Cruiser LC300 is a full-size luxury SUV that blends robust off-road capability with premium comfort. Built on a strong platform and designed to tackle tough terrain while offering a refined cabin, it carries forward the legendary Land Cruiser heritage.

Key Features

  • Full-time 4WD system with advanced off-road technologies (e.g., multi-terrain select)
  • Powerful V6 twin-turbo engine delivering strong performance and towing capacity
  • Luxurious interior with high-quality materials and advanced infotainment system
  • Comprehensive safety features including Toyota Safety Sense suite
  • Spacious seating for up to eight passengers with configurable cargo space
  • Modern exterior design with rugged yet sophisticated styling cues
  • Advanced suspension system for a smooth ride on and off-road
